The Centre for Economics and Business Research

Vision:

’’Contribute Significantly to a Saudi Economics’ Development’’

Explore more

To date, Saudi Arabia is still missing out on the positive societal, economic and policy impacts that research centre can deliver and is instead largely dominated by international consultancies, which are not necessarily rooted in the local context or have the mandate or capacity to follow up on the implementation of their recommendations. Having Saudi research canter with Saudi Academic would allow the government to establish long-term partnerships with home-grown institutes, which in turn re-invest in further enhancing local capacity
